Rigid Body problem

rem: Rigid Body problem

\[ I_1 \dot{y}_1 = (I_2-I_3) \ y_2 \ y_3 \\ I_2 \dot{y}_2 = (I_3-I_1) \ y_3 \ y_1 \\ I_3 \dot{y}_3 = (I_1-I_2) \ y_1 \ y_2 + f(t)\\ f(t) = \begin{cases} 0.25 \sin^2 t & \quad \text{if } 3 \pi \le t \le 4 \pi \\ 0 & \quad \text{otherwise} \end{cases} \]

Reference:

  1. E. Hairer, S.P. Norsett, G. Wanner, Solving Ordinary Differential Equations I - Nonstiff Problems, Springer Series in Computational Mathematics, page 244.
