Infinite horizontal optimization

rem: Infinite horizontal optimization

Maximize

\[ J[u] = \int_0^\infty e^{-t} \Big( \sin \big(u(t)\big) + \sin \big(\sqrt{2}u(t)\big) \Big) \ \mathrm{d}t \]

subjected to \(u : [0,\infty) \rightarrow \mathbb{R}\) be absolutely continuous with \( u(0) = 0\) and \(\dot{u}(t) \in [-1,1]\).
