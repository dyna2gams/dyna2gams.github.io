Bagley-Torvik equation

rem: Bagley-Torvik equation

The fractional Bagley-Torvik equation is of the form \[ A_1 \mathcal{D}^{2}x(t) + A_2 \mathcal{D}^{\nu}x(t) + A_3 x(t) = f(t) \\ 0 \leq t \leq t_f \\ 1 < \nu < 2 \] subject to the initial conditions \[ x(0) = \alpha_0, \quad \dot{x}(0) = \alpha_1 \] or the boundary conditions \[ x(0) = \beta_0, \quad x(t_f) = \beta_1 \] where \(A_1, A_2, A_3, \alpha_0, \alpha_1, \beta_0, \beta_1\) are constants with \(A_1 \neq 0\).

Reference:

  1. F. Mohammadi and S.T. Mohyud-Din, A fractional-order Legendre collocation method for solving the Bagley-Torvik equations, Advances in Difference Equations, 2016:269, 2016.
