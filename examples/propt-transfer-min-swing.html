Transfer Min Swing

rem: Transfer Min Swing

Find \(u\) over \(t \in [0,1]\) to minimize \[ J = \int_0^{1} 4.5 (x_3^2 + x_6^2) \ \mathrm{d}t \] subject to: \[ \dot{x}_1 = 9 x_4 \\ \dot{x}_2 = 9 x_5 \\ \dot{x}_3 = 9 x_6 \\ \dot{x}_4 = 9 (x_7+17.2656 x_3) \\ \dot{x}_5 = 9 x_8 \\ \dot{x}_6 = -9 \frac{x_7+27.0756 x_3+2 x_5 x_6}{x_2} \\ \dot{x}_7 = 9 u_1 \\ \dot{x}_8 = 9 u_2 \\ x(0) = [0 \ 22 \ 0 \ 0 \ -1 \ 0 \ \phi_1 \ \phi_2] \\ x(1) = [10 \ 14 \ 0 \ 2.5 \ 0 \ 0] \\ |x_4| \leq 2.5 \\ |x_5| \leq 1 \\ |x_7| \leq 2.83374 \\ -0.80865 \leq x_8 \leq 0.71265 \\ |u| \leq 10 \]

Reference:

  1. K.L. Teo, K.K. Leong, and G.J. Goh, Nonlinearly constrained optimal control problems involving piecewise smooth controls, The Journal of the Australian Mathematical Society. Series B. Applied Mathematics, pp. 151-179 (1990).

Source: PROPT - Matlab Optimal Control Software, User Manual, April 26, 2010

obj:
