Catalyst Mixing Problem

rem: Catalyst Mixing Problem

The Catalyst mixing problem seeks an optimal policy for mixing two catalysts along the length of a tubular plug of reactor involving several reactions.

Reference:

  1. Xinggao Liu, Long Chen, Yunqing Hu, Solution of Chemical Dynamic Optimization Using the Simultaneous Strategies, Chinese Journal of Chemical Engineering, Volume 21, Issue 1, January 2013, Pages 55-63.

Optimal solutions (analytical results): \[ t_f = 1, J^* = 0.0480557 \\ t_f = 12, J^* = 0.476946 \] 

par:
  tf = 1 

dyn:
  x1 x2
  u

lim:
  0 <= u <= 1

t=t0:
  x1 = 1
  x2 = 0

equ:
  x1´ == u*(10*x2-x1)
  x2´ == u*(x1-10*x2) - (1-u)*x2

obj: 
  maximize 1-final(x1+x2) using nlp
