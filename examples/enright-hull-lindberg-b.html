Oregonator equation

rem: Oregonator equation

The Oregonator, the famous model with a periodic solution describing the Belousov-Zhabotinskii reaction (Field & Noyes 1974, Enright & Hull 1976)

Reference:

  1. W.H. Enright, T.E. Hull, B. Lindberg, Comparing Numerical Methods For Stiff Systems of ODEs, BIT Numerical Mathematics, 15(1):10-48, March 1975. 

par:
  tf = 30
  
dyn:
  x{1:3}
  
lim:
  -10 <= x{1:3} <= 10

t=t0:
  x1 = 1
  x2 = 2
  x3 = 3

equ:
  x1´ == 77.27*(x2+x1*(1-8.375e-6*x1-x2))
  x2´ == (x3-(1+x1)*x2)/77.27
  x3´ == 0.161*(x1-x3)

obj:
  minimize final(x3) using nlp	# dummy objective
