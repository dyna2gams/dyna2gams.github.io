Pleiades problem

rem: Pleiades problem

\[ \ddot{x}_i = \sum_{j \ne i} m_j (x_j-x_i)/r_{ij} \\ \ddot{y}_i = \sum_{j \ne i} m_j (y_j-y_i)/r_{ij} \\ r_{ij} = ((x_i-x_j)^2 + (y_i-y_j)^2)^{3/2} \]

Reference:

  1. E. Hairer, S.P. Norsett, G. Wanner, Solving Ordinary Differential Equations I - Nonstiff Problems, Springer Series in Computational Mathematics, page 244.

exp:
  r(i,j) == (sqr(x[i]-x[j]) + sqr(y[i]-y[j]))^(3/2)

sim:
  x´[i] := v[i]
  y´[i] := w[i]
  v´[i] := sum(j$(not sameas(j,i)), ord(j)*(x[j]-x[i])/r(i,j))
  w´[i] := sum(j$(not sameas(j,i)), ord(j)*(y[j]-y[i])/r(i,j))
